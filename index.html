Intoduction To Solar

In the contemporary era, solar systems come in diverse types and models. Essentially, a solar system represents a cutting-edge method for producing electricity at one's own location. It is referred to by various names such as a solar power plant, solar photovoltaic system, solar energy system, and more. A solar system is an amalgamation of several components, including solar panels, solar inverters, solar batteries, solar charge controllers, ACDB/DCDB, and various other solar accessories. These systems prove to be a blessing for individuals grappling with frequent power outages or high electricity bills

Types Of Solar Systems

A Roadmap to Your Sustainable
Energy Future.

On-Grid Solar System

Also known as a grid-tie solar system, it utilizes solar panels and a standard on-grid solar inverter. This type doesn't require a solar battery to operate, making it relatively straightforward to install and cost-effective. However, a drawback is that in the event of a power outage, the system automatically shuts down for safety reasons. Linemen working on the grid need assurance that no other source is feeding into it. Although it doesn't generate electricity during outages, users can incorporate storage options, such as solar batteries, if desired.

This type of solar system is best alternative for those who are suffering from heavy electricity bills on regular basis and want to get rid of them. An on grid solar system also comes with net metering feature which allows its user to export access electricity to the grid.

Off-Grid Solar System

An off grid solar system is also known as stand-alone or battery based solar system. It is basically a composition of solar panel, solar inverter and solar battery as well. It is a best alternative source of energy for those who can’t easily connect to the grid due to some geographical reasons. An off grid solar system reduces our dependency on traditional utility grid and makes us self-dependent.

This solar system provides backup option to its user. The access electricity can be stored in solar battery for further use. Another major feature of an off grid solar system is that they are modular and the capacity of these solar system can easily be increase as per the power requirement.

You always have to decide the capacity of solar system according to your load requirement as it cannot run load beyond the limit. An off grid solar system can easily installed with low budget and small capacity and can be expand over time.

Hybrid Solar System

A hybrid solar system is basically a mixture of on grid solar system and off grid solar system. It is a grid-tie solar system with battery backup and net-metering. This system is specially designed for those who want the features of both solar system together.

This solar system allows you to store access generation of electricity into the solar battery. And when your solar battery is also fully charged, you can export it to the utility grid through net-metering. A hybrid solar system will provide you double benefit as it provides back up for essential loads such as lights, fan and other appliances when the grid power is out. On the other hand it also have the ability to reduce your electricity bill upto 100%.

A hybrid solar system is little bit more expensive compare to on grid solar system and off grid solar system. The addition of solar battery increase the cost of this solar system. It also have more components than other type of solar system.
